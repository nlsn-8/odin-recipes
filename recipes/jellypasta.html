Jellypasta

Description

Think out of the box and impress your friends with this original meal.

Ingredients
  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • Jello gelatin mix – Whatever flavor you want.
  • ½ teaspoon salt.
  • Tomato sauce.
Steps
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Pour the powdered gelatin into a medium-sized mixing bowl. Add boiling water to the gelatin mix, and stir for 2 minutes until it's completely dissolved.
  3. Fold macaroni into the gelatin mix while it's hot, add cold water and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until the gelatin is firm and doesn't stick to your fingers when touched.
  4. Add tomato sauce when you take it out of the refrigerator and it's done.
